# Ads Revenue Prediction Model (Sri Lanka)

## Overview
This project looks at how businesses in Sri Lanka can improve their revenue using Google Ads and YouTube Ads instead of relying only on platforms like Facebook and Instagram.

## Objective
- Understand how digital ads affect sales
- Identify which platforms bring better results
- Suggest a simple AI-based idea to estimate revenue growth

## Key Insights
- Google Ads reach people who are already searching for something
- YouTube Ads can reach a wider audience with less competition locally
- Businesses like cafes, salons, gyms, and real estate can benefit the most

## Model Idea
The idea is to estimate possible revenue growth based on:
- Ad budget
- Target audience
- Previous sales

Example:
Spending around Rs.30,000 on ads could lead to an estimated 15–20% increase in revenue.

## Marketing Approach
- Basic customer targeting methods
- How to present ad strategies to clients
- Managing and adjusting ad budgets
